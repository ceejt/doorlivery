How, what, and where to order?

Details

To order in Doorlivery Express, you must find the CATEGORY you want, such as food, school supplies, or printing. After that, you will be redirected to the MENU page. In here, type in your specific location, including the building name, floor, and room number, then proceed in browsing through the menu. Choose your desired category and stores as well as the mode of payment, may it be CASH ON DELIVERY, GCASH, or PAYMAYA. Lastly, proceed to checkout. What are you waiting for? Your order is right in front of your door!
